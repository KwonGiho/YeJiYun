War of split

UX planning

Date
December - January , 2015
Platform
iOS
Tasks
Competitive research
Survey
User flow
Usability Test
UX Planning

Summary

- Develop new functions and concept for split group application not just calculation but also enjoyment

Problem


1. Current services that did not include both communication and accounting function simultaneously decrease continuous usage on intention
2. It just managed by one-time event that makes users always have to search about history
3. There is no demand notice service for the person who manage split

Process


1. Competitive Analysis

2. User flow

3. Theoretical Research

- Using peer pressure and gamification we can make people payoff more easily.
- If people have rewards or penalty, they try to figure out more effective or fast way.

Solution

Group driven and event driven management increase continuous usage on intention

2. Motivate other members register and use, shared official fund details based on group

3. Motivate users pay off not just sending demand notice but using competitive race game page

Lesson learned


Icons and standards might not be aligned with users. Before the release or design details, we have to do usability test.

Summary & Impacts

- Enjoyment, credibility and Revisit rate increase in informational redesign page
- Improve exploratory experience by enhancing social network function

Problem


I used Youtube everyday, however their recommendation system seems limit contents rather than extend exploration in contents. The vision of Youtube is to provide a forum for people to connect, inform, and inspire others across the globe. My team want to redesign circular process

Process


1. Pre-interview

- Users do not much use recommendation system according to interview only 1 of 9 user use frequently
- Same recommended contents are repeatedly showing

2. Theoretical Research

Initial persona was just middle and high school student. Clients thought real customer who pay for this app was parents. Based on their user profile data and complain center review, persona redefinition is prior challenge.

- Informational motivated user

User who searches solution or wants to get new information from other people

-Enjoyment motivated user

User who does not have clear purpose but want to enjoy

-Hypothesis 1.

New MyPage will bring more satisfaction and enjoyment to the users compared to the original main page of YouTube

-Hypothesis 2.

There will be an interaction between different type of users and different type of pages.

-3. Experiment

-4. Survey & In depth interview

- Enjoyment, Satisfaction, Revisit intention, credibility measurement
- Conducted interview about prefer page, pros and cons and expected functions

-Findings
Informational page is the most prefer page than other two pages Informational(5), Social(3), Original(1)
